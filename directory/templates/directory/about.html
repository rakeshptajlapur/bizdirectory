About Us

Your trusted local business directory connecting customers with verified businesses across Karnataka

FindNearBiz.com (operated by Kirti Directory) is a digital platform dedicated to empowering local businesses and making it easy for customers to discover trusted services in their area. We bridge the gap between offline businesses and online customers through our simple, user-friendly directory.

Our Mission

  • Bridge the gap between customers and businesses through a simple, user-friendly online directory
  • Support MSMEs and local entrepreneurs by giving them affordable digital visibility
  • Help businesses showcase their products, services, and contact details effectively
  • Build trust through verified business listings and authentic customer reviews

Our Vision

  • Become the most reliable digital platform for local business discovery in North Karnataka
  • Enable businesses to expand their reach and connect with more customers
  • Make it easy for customers to find trusted, verified local services
  • Expand coverage across Karnataka and beyond

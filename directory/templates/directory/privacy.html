Privacy Policy

FindNearBiz (Kirti Directory) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, email, phone number when you create an account
  • Business details when you submit a listing
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Messages and reviews you submit

Non-Personal Information:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and device information
  • Usage data and website analytics
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage business listings
  • Communicate with you regarding your account or listing
  • Improve our services, user experience, and security
  • Send promotional updates (only if you opt-in)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform safety
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment gateways, email services) for business operations
  • Business Listings: Information you choose to make public in your business listing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of our business

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to personalize your experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze how you use our website
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Improve our services and user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure servers and encrypted data transmission
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and staff training
  • Secure payment processing

However, no online platform is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee complete protection against all threats.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Update: Correct or update your personal information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain types of data processing

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law to retain certain data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
